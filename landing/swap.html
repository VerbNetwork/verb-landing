Streaming Swaps

Patient Swappers Pay Less Fees

Streaming Swaps break up larger swaps into multiple smaller sub-swaps executed over a period of time

Price Optimized Swaps

Users can pick the number of blocks between sub-swaps and the number of sub-swaps to execute. More sub-swaps means paying less liquidity fees.

Better Execution

Streaming swap fees are so low ecause liquidity demanbds for a trade are met over time, rather than all at once. Swap fees can go as low as 5 basis points, even for the largest trades

On-Chain TWAP

THORChain has built-in MEV protection and deterministic swap ordering to make it safe trading over an extended period of time.

Synthetic Assets

Trade Synths on THORChain

In addition to cross-chain swaps, THORChain offers synthetics for the fast, efficient, and cheap trading of assets on any supported network.

Fast Transactions

Synthetics benefit from THORChain’s fast block times and near instant finality. Trade any supported asset in seconds.

Better Prices

Faster and cheaper arbitrage results in better prices for all users on the exchange.

Backed by Liquidity

Every synth is backed by liquidity in THORChain's pools to guarantee they are always 1:1 redeemable for the underlying asset (before fees)
