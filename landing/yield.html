Savers

Earn with THORChain Savers

Transparent Yields

Savers earn yield from every swap. At the end of each block, half of the yield generated is used to buy assets and donate to Savers.

Low Risk Liquidity

No impermanent loss or RUNE price exposure. Savers are built using synthetic assets and backed by the liquidity pools. Savers are the most risk-averse way to provide liquidity to THORChain.

Withdraw Anytime

THORChain is permissionless to deposit and withdraw. Only your key can be used to move your funds. Savers can withdraw their position to native assets at any time.

Liquidity Provision

Become a Liquidity Provider

More Yield

Liquidity Providers earn double the yield of Savers in the same pool. Liquidity providers insure Savers from impermanent loss, so they earn extra yield.

Earn From Every Swap

Liquidity providers earn liquidity fees from every swap that takes place on THORChain in addition to block rewards paid from the Reserve.

Withdraw Anytime

THORChain is permissionless to deposit and withdraw. Only your key can be used to move your funds. Liquidity providers can withdraw their position to native assets at any time.
